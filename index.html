Core Features

Everything Your Society Needs, Built In

From billing to complaints, every workflow your committee already runs — organized, tracked, and transparent.

User & Access Management

Admin can create, edit, deactivate, and manage house owner accounts with secure role-based access for Super Admin, Committee/Treasurer, and House Owner.

Maintenance Billing

Auto-generate monthly or quarterly maintenance bills with configurable rules — flat rate, per sq. ft., or slab-based billing.

Online Payments

Residents can pay maintenance using UPI, card, net banking, or bank transfer, with automatic digital receipts generated after payment.

Manual Payment Entry

Committee members can record cash, cheque, or bank transfer payments manually with full receipt tracking.

Expense Tracking

Log society expenses by category, upload invoices, manage vendor payments, and maintain clean financial records.

Financial Dashboard & Reports

View total collected, pending dues, expenses, current balance, monthly trends, and export reports in PDF or Excel.

Member Transparency View

Residents get read-only access to their own payment history, pending dues, expense summary, and the society balance overview.

Complaints / Ticket System

Residents can raise complaints with photos and track status as Open, In Progress, or Resolved.

Notices & Announcements

Admins can publish important society notices, meeting updates, payment reminders, and announcements for all members.

Polls & Voting

Conduct society polls for AGM decisions, maintenance approvals, events, or day-to-day community voting.

Audit Log

Every edit, payment update, bill change, expense modification, and admin action is tracked with user name, date, time, old value, and new value.
